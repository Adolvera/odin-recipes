Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

This easy-to-make old-school macaroni and cheese has a perfectly crispy crust.

This macaroni and cheese recipe will take you back to your childhood days! With no worry in making an accident, this recipe will have you coming back to way too often.

Ingredients

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon white pepper
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 panko bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat
  3. Once boiling, stir in macaroni, and return to boil
  4. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is cooked ghrough but still firm, about 8 minutes
  5. Drain well
  6. Melt butter into large saucepan
  7. When foaming and bubbling, stir in flour
  8. Cook on medium heat until flour begins to turn pale yellow, 3 to 4 minutes
  9. Add thyme, cayenne pepper, and white pepper, cook and stir another minute
  10. Whisk in 1 cup of milk until smooth
  11. Pour remaining milk and whisk again, bring to simmer
  12. Stir in nutmeg, Worcestershire sauce, and salt
  13. SImmer on medium-low heat until thickened, 8 minutes, whisking often
  14. Turn heat off, then add 2 1/4 cups of Chedder cheese
  15. Stir until melted and combined, add Dijon mustard
  16. Transfer the macaroni into a casserole dish, then pour in cheese sauce
  17. stir thoroughly combine sauce with pasta
  18. Mix panko bread crumbs with 1 tablespoon melted butter in small bowl, and sprinkle crumbs on top of macaroni and cheese
  19. Sprinkle remaining 3/4 cups of Cheddar cheese on top
  20. Bake until golden brown on top, about 20 minutes
