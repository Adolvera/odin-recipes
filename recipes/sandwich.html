Cuban Midnight Sandwich

This Cuban Midnight Sandwich is perfect for a quick dinner or naughty nighttime snack

This sandwich is called a 'Media Noche' which translates to 'Midnight.' It makes a wonderful dinner sandwich because it is served hot. A nice side dish is black bean soup or black beans and rice, and plantain chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 5 tablespoons Italian dressing
  • 4 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise
  • 4 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 1/2 pound thinly sliced deli turkey meat
  • 1/2 pound thinly sliced cooked ham
  • 1/2 pound thinly sliced Swiss cheese
  • 1 cup dill pickle slices
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix together mayo and Italian dressing
  2. Spread on hoagie rolls with mustard
  3. On each roll, arrange layers of turkey, ham, and cheese
  4. Top each with dill pickle slices
  5. Close sandwich, and brush top and bottom with olive oil
  6. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium high heat
  7. Cook sandwich on each side for 2 minutes, or until cheese melted
  8. Remove from heat, plate, and cut diagonally
